## Who We Are

<section>

</section>

     {{1}}

## What Drives Our Success

At the core of everything are three words: **Passion, Integrity, Quality** — plus a promise to focus on client success.

<section>

     {{1}}
- **Passion** → our disciplined, everyday effort on behalf of clients. It's how we express *teamwork*.
- **Integrity** → woven through our culture; simply part of our DNA.
- **Quality** → ensures ongoing success for clients, partners, and teammates, proven in our results.
- **Focus on Client Success** → our promise to be responsive, disciplined, consistent, and relevant.

</section>

<article>

     {{2}}
**Our framework: the Adaptable Data Center®️ (ADC)**

We believe the "data center" isn't a place — it's a set of capabilities that deliver business outcomes. The ADC framework lets us simplify and scale complex technologies (AI, security, hybrid cloud, containers, data management) while removing technical debt.

     {{3}}
A guiding tenet runs through all of it: **"Security-In-Everything"** — built in from the first design decision, not bolted on later.

</article>
